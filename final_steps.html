Final Steps

Infographic

Now it's your turn to create an infographic in groups and each group will be in charge of one aspect/section of the infographic. Here are the steps:

1. Groups of 5 people (chosen by the teacher heterogeneously)

2. Assign roles among the groups.

-1 group will be in charge of Poe's life.

-1 group will be in charge of Poe's historial period. 

-1 group will be in charge of Poe's literature period.

-1 group will be in charge of Poe's works.

-1 group will be in charge of Poe's repercussions nowadays in present literature and his influence. 

3. Use Canva to create an infographic about your chosen work. It will be a collaborative work that you have to complete on one single template.
